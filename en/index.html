Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lingflix? +
Lingflix is a mobile app that helps you learn foreign languages by watching videos with dual interactive subtitles. You can watch any YouTube video or local video file with subtitles in both your target language and your native language, clicking on any word for instant translation.
How does the dual subtitle feature work? +
Lingflix displays two subtitle lines simultaneously - one in the language you're learning and one in your native language. You can click on any word in either subtitle to see its translation and add it to your personal vocabulary list for later review.
What languages are supported? +
Lingflix supports 68 languages! The app is localized for 68 languages, meaning you can use it in your native language regardless of which language you're learning. All combinations are supported - learn any language from any language.
Can I use my own video files? +
Yes! In addition to YouTube videos, Lingflix allows you to watch local video files stored on your device. Simply load any movie or video from your phone or tablet, and you can watch it with dual interactive subtitles.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes! Lingflix offers a 7-day free trial so you can explore all features before committing. No credit card is required to start your trial.
What devices can I use Lingflix on? +
Lingflix is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can use it on your iPhone, iPad, Android phone, or Android tablet.
How does the vocabulary builder work? +
Every word you click on while watching videos is automatically saved to your personal dictionary. You can review these words later and use them for memorization and practice, helping you retain what you've learned.
Can I find content in any subject area? +
Absolutely! You can watch any YouTube video on any topic, from cooking and science to sports and entertainment. Lingflix provides curated playlists for all languages, but you can also use YouTube search or paste any video URL to find content that interests you.
